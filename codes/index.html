① What is an E-waste facility locator?
  An E-waste facility locator is an online tool or application that helps users find authorized facilities for the disposal and recycling of electronic waste in their vicinity.








② Why is it important to use an E-waste facility locator?
Using an E-waste facility locator is important because it promotes responsible disposal of electronic waste, preventing environmental harm and supporting sustainable practices.


③ How do I use the E-waste facility locator?
Typically, you can search for nearby e-waste facilities by entering your location.











④ Are there any fees associated with using e-waste facilities listed on the locator?
Information about any fees for e-waste disposal should be available in the facility profiles. Some facilities may charge fees for certain types of electronic waste.
